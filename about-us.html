About Us

House of Arts: is working as Buying Agency for Rugs, Textiles and Decorative Metals since 2005, having Family Roots in Business since last 55 years.

Presently, House of Arts is working with renowned companies in Europe, US Australia; we have the privilege to work with the best in the Trade.

Sourcing and Procurement are not just words and profile, it’s an elusive art which comes with passage of time spent in the trade, we have roots which are really strong with Rugs as our company originates in Varanasi, biggest hub and centre for Handmade Rug, manufacturers are in Bhadohi, Khamaria, Mirzapur ... and having our office based in Varanasi helps us to be in the direct vicinity of our Suppliers.

We opened new Wings in Delhi (Gurgaon) in 2014, taking care after the Textile and Decorative Metal Divisions. All our Suppliers are having great Export Histories and are well equipped to cater any demand or quality, we are working with set of companies who are having all the export compliances and are enrolled with Care & Fare or Good Weave . We are working with large sets of Suppliers Specialising is Different qualities, it’s a blend of Suppliers engaged in Operations with likes of Wallmart, Ikea, Crate & Barrel, Pottery Barn and So on –
